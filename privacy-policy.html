3. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Pixle, we may collect the following categories of information.

We may automatically collect technical information about your device, including:

  • device model;
  • operating system and version;
  • application version;
  • preferred language;
  • country or region;
  • time zone;
  • screen resolution;
  • anonymous device identifiers;
  • advertising identifiers provided by your operating system, where applicable.

This information helps us ensure compatibility, improve stability, and optimize the application for different devices.

Gameplay Information

To provide game functionality, we may process gameplay-related information such as:

  • completed puzzle levels;
  • current game progress;
  • unlocked achievements;
  • Daily Challenge progress;
  • hint usage;
  • Pro purchase status;
  • gameplay statistics used for analytics.

Gameplay information does not contain information that directly identifies you.

Analytics Information

We collect anonymous or pseudonymous analytics information to understand how Pixle is used. Examples include:

  • application launches;
  • session duration;
  • completed levels;
  • feature usage;
  • Daily Challenge activity;
  • advertisement interactions;

  • general performance information.

Analytics help us improve gameplay, identify issues, and enhance future updates.

Purchase Information

If you purchase Pixle Pro, the purchase itself is processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store.

We do not receive or store your payment card information.

We only receive information necessary to verify your purchase status.

Contact Information

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • your email address;
  • your message;
  • any attachments you choose to include.

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry or provide support.
