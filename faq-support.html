FAQ & Support

Help, questions and contact

Q

How do I play Pixle?

Drag and drop puzzle pieces onto the board to reconstruct the original image. Completed pieces snap into place automatically.

You can use the preview button at any time to see what the finished puzzle should look like.

Q

What is Daily Challenge?

The Daily Challenge is a new seasonal puzzle that refreshes regularly. Complete it to keep your streak and earn bonus rewards.

Daily puzzles are the same for every player on the same day.

Q

How do hints work?

Hints highlight a piece and its correct position on the board. You have a limited number of hints available per puzzle.

Use them wisely — they cannot be refilled during an active puzzle.

Q

Why do I see ads?

Ads help keep Pixle free for everyone. They only appear in the free version between puzzles and never during active gameplay.

To remove ads, consider upgrading to Pixle Pro.

Q

How can I contact support?

You can reach us by email at any time. We aim to respond within 2–3 business days.

Please include your device model and app version when reporting an issue.
